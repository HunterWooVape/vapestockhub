# Inventory Page SEO Spec

Route：`/inventory`

## Role
- 主库存类目页。
- 承接“我要看一批可选货源”的批发采购意图。
- 负责把买家分发到详情页、价格页、品牌页、市场页和询盘渠道。

## Primary Keyword
`wholesale disposable vapes`

## Secondary Keywords
- `bulk disposable vapes`
- `disposable vape wholesale`
- `disposable vapes bulk`
- `wholesale disposable vape stock`（页面语义，不作为主获客词）

## Metadata
- Title：`Wholesale Disposable Vapes in Bulk | VapeStockHub`
- Description：`Browse wholesale disposable vapes in bulk by brand, market, price range, MOQ, and warehouse location. Compare active stock offers and request live price and availability via Telegram or WhatsApp.`

## H1
`Wholesale Disposable Vapes in Bulk`

## Top Intro
`Explore active disposable vape offers for wholesale and bulk buyers. Filter by brand, market, warehouse, and price visibility, then open a listing to confirm MOQ, live price, available quantity, and shipping-ready stock.`

## UX / Copy Adjustments
- `Search inventory` 改为 `Search wholesale stock`。
- Filters 描述加入 `brand, market, warehouse`。
- 结果数量下方文案加入 `bulk disposable vapes` 和 `MOQ`。
- FAQ 增加 `cheap disposable vapes` 到 `/price/under-3` 的内链入口。
- FAQ 增加 warehouse / local stock 问答。
- Query 参数页继续 `noindex`，clean `/inventory` 承担主 SEO。

## FAQ Direction
- `Can I source wholesale disposable vapes in bulk from this page?`
- `How do I compare MOQ, quantity, and wholesale price?`
- `Can I find cheap disposable vapes here?`
- `Do listings include warehouse location or local stock?`
- `How do I request live availability?`

## Warehouse
- Warehouse 是筛选、搜索、FAQ、卡片和详情页字段。
- 当前不开发 `/warehouse/[slug]`。
